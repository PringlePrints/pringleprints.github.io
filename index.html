02 · File specs

Preparing your file

Formats
PDF (XML job tickets also accepted). If you're unsure whether your file will work, send it anyway — we'll check it before printing and follow up if anything needs adjusting.
Resolution
300 DPI or higher for crisp results.
Bleed
If your layout includes bleed and cut lines, we'll follow them. If not, we'll confirm trim with you before cutting.
Delivery
Attach your PDF or XML file directly on the order form. Have an unusually large file or a different format? Email us at orders@pringleprints.ca first and we'll sort out the best way to send it.

04 · What happens next

After you order

We'll confirm your order and file by email, flag anything that needs fixing before it goes to print, and let you know the timeline. Pay by card or Interac e-Transfer right at checkout. Printing and hand-cutting a batch takes real time — we'll always give you an honest estimate rather than a fast one.

We currently serve the Ottawa–Gatineau region for pickup and local delivery. Canada-wide shipping is on the way — if you're elsewhere in Canada, get in touch anyway and we'll see what we can work out.
